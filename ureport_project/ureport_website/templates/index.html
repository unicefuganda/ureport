About Ureport

Ureport is a free SMS-based system that allows young Ugandans to speak out on what's happening in communities across the country, and work together with other community leaders for positive change.

Read more about the project

Why join Ureport

  • Speak out on what's happening in your community
  • Use Ureport results and information to update and increase citizen's awareness
  • Participate in nationwide campaigns to help improve your country
  • Share what's happening around Uganda with other UReporters
